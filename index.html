Houston Underground

...is the largest - and most awesome - amateur saxophone competition for high school and junior high students in the country. Each year, our Quartet, Jr. High Quartet, and Soloist contests attract more than 35 quartets and over 80 soloists from more than 200 miles away from Houston.

Judges

Judges for Houston Underground are professional saxophonists, known throughout the world for their playing and pedaogy. Past judges include Nathan Nabb, Lynn Klock, Stephen Page, Otis Murphy, Jess Voigt-Page, Stephen Banks, and many more!
Every participant recieves hand-written commentary from each of the three judges, providing performers with incredibly valuable and customized feedback of their playing.

Prizes

Over $1500 in prize money and thousands of cutting-edge industry products are given away as prizes every year. Multiple prizes are available in every category, plus specialty prizes like the People's Choice and Judges' Honorable Mention. In adiditon, every participant is given take-home product like reeds, mouthpieces, and sponsor swag.
